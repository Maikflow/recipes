Ingredients:
    2 Eggs
    1/2 cup of skimmed milk
    100 grams of cocoa
    1 teaspoon of coffee
    2 teaspoons of sugar
    1 spoon of whipped cream

Preparation:
    Mix 2 eggs, cofee, sugar and milk and stir
    til you achieve brown batter.

    Put the brown batter in low fire while stirring
    constansly. 

    After achieving a whiter brown batter, take away from the
    stove and then put it in the freezer for about a day.

    The next day Take out your semifredo and put the whipped
    cream on top while sprinkling some cofee for appearance.

    And voila, you have a mocca semifredo ready.
